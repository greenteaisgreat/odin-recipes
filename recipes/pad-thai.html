Pad Thai


Pad thai noodles garnished with cilanto and peanuts

This quick and easy recipe will satisfy even the pickiest of eaters.
With a simple prep and short cook-time, this recipe is a no-brainer!

Ingredients

  1. 1 (12 ounce) package of rice noodles
  2. 2 tablespoons butter
  3. 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-sized pieces
  4. 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  5. 4 eggs
  6. 1 tablespoon white vinegar
